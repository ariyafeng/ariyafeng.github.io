About Me

Hi, I'm Ariya (pronounced uh-ree-yuh).

I grew up in Maryland and am currently a sophomore at Cornell University in Ithaca, New York, where I am studying Information Science and minoring in Design and Environmental Analysis. My first year of college was spent at the University of Maryland, College Park studying Computer Science.

I transferred to Cornell because I wanted to pursue a degree that could combine my passions for technology, design, and the environment. My concentration in user experience design and my minor allow me to do just that. I hope to use technology and design to create something that can better the world we live in.
