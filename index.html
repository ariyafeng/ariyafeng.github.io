What Makes Me Beautiful

What Makes Me Beautiful is a children's book I wrote and illustrated in 2017 as part of B'nai B'rith's Diverse Minds Writing Challenge. My book was written with a message of promoting diversity and equality among everyone, regardless of race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or abilities.

Published copies were donated to local libaries in Washington D.C. I hope the readers of my story will be able to see themselves in the characters and realize what makes them special.
