What Makes Me Beautiful

What Makes Me Beautifulis a children's book I wrote and illustrated in 2017.

As part of B'nai B'rith's   Diverse Minds Writing Challenge,my book was written with a message of promoting diversity and equality among everyone, regardless of race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or abilities.

I spent a summer at the National Institute for Standards and Technology (NIST). My job was to develop and design a   website dedicated to microbial standards in order to provide references for people outside of NIST to use.

My first real-life project in web design! Albeit, powered by WordPress. It was still fun to explore and incorporate website languages that I've learned into it. The current website has had a few updates since I worked on it last, but main components remained the same.

About me

Hi, I'm Ariya (pronounced uh-ree-yuh). I grew up in Maryland and currently attend Cornell University in Ithaca, New York. I am studying Policy Analysis and Management and minoring in Design and Environmental Analysis.

My interests in policy actually came from writing my children's book and seeing the power that words had on people. As for design, I've always had a love for art and drawing and see design as a way of connecting people and art through real-life applications. And I care about the environment. A lot. So what could be a better combination than policy and environmental analysis?
