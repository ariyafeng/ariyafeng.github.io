CivicRX

CivicRX is an initiative started by Dr. Sejal Hathi to increase voter turnout in the 2020 general election.

Surprisingly, doctors have one of the lowest voter turnouts in the United States. Paired with the fact that health policies in the US are increasingly profit-driven, public trust in medicine is ebbing. Physicians have not been vocal in representing their patients perspectives, and CivicRX is actively working to combat this.

CivicRX and TurboVote have teamed together in efforts to increase voter turnout during a critical election that is taking place in the midst of a pandemic.
